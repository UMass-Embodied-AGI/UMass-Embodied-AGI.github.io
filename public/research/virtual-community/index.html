VirtualCommunity: A Generative Social World for Embodied AI

Video Demo

Below we present some demo videos of humanoid agents and robots walking and interacting in different geolocations around the world generated by our pipeline.

Agents are provided the interface to communicate and interact with each other and scenes based on a physics engine. Each agent has its own planning system and has generated an identity profile to support social interaction at scale.
